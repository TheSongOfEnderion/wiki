[[toc]] The history of Mirdum in chronological order. * [[Eternal Era]] - Approximately 500,000 years ago. An extremely advanced era of magic, science, and technology where gods walk the lands, where masters of sword or magic is as common as grains of sand, and where Madness itself is the air one breaths. Elves, Orcs, Dwarves, and Dragons walked the land. However, these races became extinct. * [[Mythical Era]] - Also known as the Great Migration. Races from all over the world conjoined and traveled to [[Loreteia]] to seek new lands. It is the era before the first era where Loreteia is poisoned by Blue Mist, creating a post-apocalyptic civilization. ** [[UE 1]] - The [[Mad Scholar]] goes on a journey to find the [[Legendary Bloom Doom Shroom]], a legendary bloom jelly ingredient so miraculous it is said to give, "The Greatest Bloom in all of Existence". ** [[UE 1.8]] - The [[Ancient Heroes]] were formed, a rag-tag group of lunatics whom went on a Journey to liberate the lands from evil Eldras. ** [[UE 2]] - The [[Bloom Lotus Company]] was founded. ** [[UE 2.5]] - While researching ancient technology, the [[Mad Scholar]] translated the [[Termo Eternalis]] where it detailed research about the Fusion Engine. ** [[UE 4]] - The [[Mad Scholar]] was fucking around with the Chaos Titan, the [[World Serpent]] and accidentally sent it forward in time before he and his friends were about to kill it. ** [[UE 4.3]] - The [[Librarium]] was created by the [[Bloom Lotus Company]]. ** [[UE 5]] - The [[Eternal Tower]] was awakened by the [[Madscholar]], which created a barrier around [[Mirdum]] that protected it from the invasion of [[Eldra | Eldras]]. The land goddess [[Aulindrel]] awakened. ** [[UE 5.1]] - During the awakening of the Eternal Tower, the [[Mad Scholar]] did a little trolling on [[Anessa the Saint]] and fooled her into thinking she made a covenant with the gods to keep the world safe. * [[First Era]] - Following the Awakening of the [[Eternal Tower]] by the [[Mad Scholar]], marked the beginning of the First Era. ** [[1E 203]] - Under the leadership of the [[Golden Scales]], the dravirs, conquered [[Heilgrim]] and Raekirnu, creating the [[Golden Empire]]. ** [[1E 564]] - The Golden Empire fell as the Chief King of [[Heilgrim]] rebelled, creating a massacred of Dravirs all the way to the heart of the Golden Empire. The [[Raekirnu]] people saw the bloodshed political battles brought and their Tribe Father decided that they will not ally with any more race in the future. ** [[1E 601]] - [[Luna Menur]] allied with [[Sarashi]] while [[Aulmeria]] allied with [[Heilgrim]]. The two empires fought. ** [[1E 734]] - The alliance between Luna Menur and Sarashi, and Aulmeria and Heilgrim fell to pieces. ** [[1E 1423]] - The [[Hmner]] race were wiped out because of the trolling that the [[Mad Scholar]] did after he left a note in the translation of the [[Termo Eternalis]] tricking the reader to do an erroneous move which will cause the Fusion Engine to blow up. ** [[1E 2093]] - The Maddened Sorcerer King, [[Tarnox]] cast a spell which blocked the light of the sun upon the continent of [[Loreteia]], causing the [[Vein of the Fallen God]] to awaken. It was a time where men cannot die and the dead walks everywhere. The [[Lone Warrior]] appeared during this time and liberated the lands by slaying [[Tarnox]] and destroying the [[Vein of the Fallen God]]. WIth countries all over Loreteia destroyed, after this event, it marked the end of the First Era. * [[Second Era]] - Following the destruction of the [[Vein of the Fallen God]], marked the 2nd Era. ** [[2E 403]] - The [[Vestige]] woke up into the world, brought forth by [[Fygnell]] to kill the Chaos Titan, the [[World Serpent]]. ** [[2E 1103]] - [[Euphemia | Euphemia the Maid]], a descendant of the [[Lone Warrior]] was born. ** [[2E 1121]] - [[Euphemia | Euphemia the Maid]], found the [[Ordinary Quill]]. ** [[2E 1123]] - [[Euphemia | Euphemia the Maid]] usurped the throne and became King of [[Ascaron]]. ** [[2E 1131]] - After 8 years of War, Euphemia successfully united all of [[Loreteia]]. A unification feat no one has ever achieved in the continent's history. Euphemia became [[Euphemia]], the Great Conquerer.
