In a world filled with superheroes, everyone wants to be the One to save the world. 200 years have passed since the Invasion of the Gods, known as the Eternal Crisis. Magic no longer exists and mana has been erased from the world. With 90% of the world population possessing Superpowers known as Psions, great trouble emerges as Villains and Evil-doers seems to increasingly grew each year. Ethan Morales is a smart and confident kid with a rather chaotic personality. Like any other kid, he too wanted to become a Hero. But his wish changed into something sinister after an accident a year ago. Emboldened by his thirst to take back what was stolen, Ethan enrolled into UHA, one of the Greatest Hero Academy in the world.
